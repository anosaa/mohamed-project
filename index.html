About Project

Chronic Renal Disease (CRD) is a non-communicable disease that has considerably impacted the global growth in morbidity, mortality, and the rate of patient admission. Therefore, it must be minimized by early detection using various machine learning algorithms to determine the status of chronic renal disease in its preliminary stages.
